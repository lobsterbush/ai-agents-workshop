Part 3

Where They Fail

Known failure modes

Hallucinated citations. They invent references. Verify everything.

Wrong statistics. Numbers can be subtly off. Check the math.

Overfit to your prompt. They do what you ask, even if wrong.

The verification principle

Treat output like a research assistant's first draft.

  • Run the code yourself
  • Spot-check calculations
  • Verify citations exist
  • Review the logic

Summary

Three things

  • Agents act. They execute, not just advise.
  • Verify everything. Trust requires validation.
  • Document by default. The log is your audit trail.
